Welcome to the Admin View


Here you will find detail and updated information on all of your shuttle drivers. You'll be able to use their car information to grab real-time and accurate information on both their driving etiquecy and efficiency. But instead of having you regularly check the status of all your drivers, there's an implemented auto-notification feature that can alert any drivers that may be going astray. Should they choose to ignore the notification, all admins assigned to them will be alerted that they've ignored their notification warning.
